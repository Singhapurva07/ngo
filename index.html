About Samarthya Welfare Trust

A women-led organization dedicated to creating sustainable social change through comprehensive community development programs and empowerment initiatives.

Samarthya Welfare Trust was established on 17th April 2017 by Pallavi Deep along with seven other passionate women driven by an unwavering commitment to social service and community empowerment.

Our organization stands as a shining example of women empowerment, where all 25 dedicated members are women who work tirelessly to serve those in need. We have touched thousands of lives across Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, and continue to expand our reach.

Through strategic partnerships with government initiatives, comprehensive health programs, environmental conservation efforts, and community development projects, we create sustainable, long-lasting impact.

Our Foundation

Vision • Mission • Achievement

Guided by clear principles and measurable outcomes, we're building a framework for sustainable social change that resonates across generations.

🎯

Our Vision

Creating a healthy, strong, sustainable, and aware India where empowerment, prosperity, and equality are not just ideals but lived realities. We envision communities where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to collective progress.

🚀

Our Mission

Through comprehensive awareness programs focusing on environmental conservation, natural resource protection, and sustainable development, we empower women with essential skills and knowledge to build a developed, self-reliant India that serves as a model for the world.

🏆

Our Recognition

Proudly certified by NITI Aayog with 12A & 80G recognition, validating our commitment to transparency, accountability, and measurable impact. This recognition empowers us to scale our initiatives and attract strategic partnerships for greater social change.
